# Customer_Lifetime_Value_Analysis
This project predicts the customer lifetime value based on the customer purchase behavior. Such application is common in the field of business intelligence

The goal of predictive CLV is to model the purchasing behavior of customers in order to infer what their future actions will be. Whether a predictive CLV model and methodology makes sense for the use case will largely be determined by the business context. Usually the business context is defined along two dimensions: non-contractual vs. contractual business settings, and continuous vs. discrete purchase opportunities.
